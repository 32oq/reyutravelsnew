About
Reyu Tour & Travels

Home • About

Reyu Tour & Travels is a prominent travel agency based in the enchanting region of Kashmir, known for its breathtaking natural beauty. Established with a passion for providing exceptional travel experiences, Reyu Tour & Travels has become a trusted name in the tourism industry. With its headquarters located in the capital city of Srinagar, Reyu Tour & Travels specializes in organizing customized tours and packages to cater to the diverse needs of travelers. Whether it's a serene family vacation, an adventurous trekking expedition, or a romantic honeymoon getaway, the company offers a wide range of services to ensure a memorable trip. One of the key strengths of Reyu Tour & Travels is its team of experienced and knowledgeable travel experts. They possess an in-depth understanding of the region and are well-versed in curating itineraries that showcase the best of Kashmir. From suggesting popular tourist destinations to arranging transportation and accommodations, the team provides comprehensive support to make each journey hassle-free. Reyu Tour & Travels takes pride in offering a variety of tour options, allowing visitors to explore the captivating landscapes of Kashmir. From the serene Dal Lake in Srinagar to the breathtaking meadows of Gulmarg and the serene valleys of Pahalgam, the company ensures that travelers get to witness the natural wonders of the region. Moreover, they also arrange visits to iconic landmarks like the Mughal Gardens, Shankaracharya Temple, and Hazratbal Shrine, providing a deeper cultural experience. Safety and comfort are paramount for Reyu Tour & Travels. They maintain a fleet of well-maintained vehicles and work with reputable hotels and houseboats to ensure a comfortable stay for their guests. Additionally, their guides are proficient in multiple languages, making communication easier for international travelers. Reyu Tour & Travels aims to create unforgettable memories for every traveler who chooses to explore Kashmir with them. With their commitment to customer satisfaction and their dedication to showcasing the region's beauty, they have earned a reputation as one of the leading travel companies in Kashmir. Whether it's the first visit or a return trip, Reyu Tour & Travels promises an extraordinary journey through the enchanting valleys and mountains of Kashmir.
